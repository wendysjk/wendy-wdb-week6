About

Wendy Seo-jin Koo ⋆ October 18 ⋆ Illustrator

Wendy Koo is an illustrator based in New Jersey and New York City. After majoring in Commerical Art at Bergen Technical High School, she entered class of 2018 for Illustration at Parsons School of Design. Heavily influenced by cartoons and Japanese manga, Wendy likes to incorporate a magical and whimsical essence within her work.
